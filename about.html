About

BOUBOP is a jewelry project rooted in play, texture, and personal expression. Each piece is handmade and exists somewhere between nostalgia, experimentation, and instinct.

The work draws from small details — beads, charms, color, asymmetry — and builds them into objects that feel intimate and slightly chaotic, but intentional.

BOUBOP is not about perfection. It is about feeling, collecting, and wearing something that feels like your own small world.
