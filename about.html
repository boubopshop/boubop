About

BOUBOP is a jewelry project rooted in play, texture, and personal expression. Each piece is handmade and exists somewhere between nostalgia, experimentation, instinct, and styling.

The work draws from small details — beads, charms, color, asymmetry — and builds them into objects that feel intimate, collected, and a little bit surreal.

BOUBOP is not about perfection. It is about shine, feeling, and wearing something that feels like a tiny world of your own.
