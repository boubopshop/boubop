Coppenhagen Collection

The Coppenhagen Collection is a series of one-of-a-kind necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and individual wire-wrapped beads and charms, created for Coppenhagen Beads in Amsterdam. Available from April 3rd, 2026, in store only.

Each piece is one of a kind, made by hand without a fixed pattern. Materials, colors, and shapes are combined intuitively, so every piece is different in its own way.
